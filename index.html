Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

Introduction

Parent Reminder ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information. The App is designed for all ages, including children under 5 years old, and does not collect any personal information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect or store any personal data. The only information used is your device location, and it is only used to calculate accurate prayer times and reminders. This information is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared.

Children's Privacy

Our App is safe for children, including those under 5 years old. We do not collect any personal details, identifiers, or sensitive data from children or adults.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Security

Since no personal data is collected, your privacy is fully protected. The location data used for prayer times remains only on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
